What is Strength in Diversity?

We bring together people from different worlds to connect, learn from each other, and elevate each other.

Our core program is a mixed-income, mixed-race community of people living together in East Palo Alto, CA and helping each other make strides in creative careers.

Why I built this

Society is unequal. This inequality leads to hatred, violence, and loss of productivity. Unequal opportunity is also morally wrong. It is unfair.

Underprivileged people suffer from an Opportunity Gap.

The secrets of the privileged world are unknown to them because the worlds of the “haves” and the “have-nots” do not overlap at all. Underprivileged people don’t even know what they are missing. To achieve success, health, and happiness--they must first gain exposure to these “unknown unknowns”.

Privileged people suffer from an Empathy Gap.

They do not understand the hopes, needs, and fears of everyone else because they live in an isolated world of elites. This ignorance leads to violence, disunity, and short-sighted decision making. These ignorant people have control over all power in today’s world. This must change. To bring about peace, unity, and equality--they must first gain empathy.
